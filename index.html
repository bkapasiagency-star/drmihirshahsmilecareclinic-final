About The Clinic

A dental clinic that doesn't feel like one.

Step inside and you'll notice it immediately: natural light, calm colours, and none of the clinical chill you brace yourself for. Since 2004, Smile Care Clinic has been Surat's trusted address for advanced, judgement-free dental care, built around strict global sterilisation standards and a genuinely welcoming environment for every family we treat.

Behind that comfort is serious technology: our operatories are equipped with soft-tissue dental lasers and two chairside intraoral scanners, so crown, bridge, and aligner work is planned digitally, precise and without the messy impression trays of old-school dentistry.

Meet Dr. Mihir R. Shah

A trusted name in Surat dentistry for over two decades.

Dr. Mihir R. Shah completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from the prestigious Government Dental College and Hospital, Jamnagar, in 2001. Across a 25+ year career, more than 20 of those years focused specifically on advanced implantology, he has planned and delivered healthy, functional, good-looking smiles for thousands of families across the city.

He is an active member of the Indian Dental Association, holding himself to global standards of dental ethics, patient safety, and clinical hygiene in every single procedure.
